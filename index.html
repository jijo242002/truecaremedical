About Us

Medical Equipment Services

True Care Medical Trading LLC specializes in comprehensive diagnostic and repair services for medical imaging equipment. We offer solutions including preventive maintenance, corrective repairs, and advanced ultrasound probe repair services. Committed to reliability and precision, we support healthcare facilities in maintaining high standards of patient care through efficient equipment management. Our dedicated team of experts ensures that each service meets rigorous standards, helping medical providers deliver accurate diagnostics while maximizing equipment longevity and performance.
